Drinks Reception/Evening Meal

Often called the reception, drinks reception, or pre-reception, Claire can be booked as a harpist, pianist, singer-harpist, or singer-pianist for the arrival and entertainment of your guests at your wedding venue. Alternatively, music can be provided to accompany your evening meal.

Perhaps you would like the elegance of classical piano, or maybe the ambience of the Irish harp. Claire plays a mixture of light classical, pop & contemporary, film music, and beautiful Irish airs. A second instrumentalist can also be provided. For example, a lively trad duo of fiddle and harp would bring a room to life!

There is a reduced rate when you book a combination of services, e.g. church music and reception music. Please contact Claire (info@claireheffernan.com) to see what she can do for you!
