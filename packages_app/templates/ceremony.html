Weddings

Claire performs weekly at wedding ceremonies, receptions, & evening meals. Any combination of these can be provided as a reduced rate package deal. Ceremonies catered for are church ceremonies, humanist, spiritual, HSE, same-sex, blessings, vow renewals… or let Claire know if you had something else in mind.

Claire can be booked as a harpist, pianist, singer, singer-harpist, or singer-pianist. She is also available to collaborate with other singers or musicians, having a wide network to call on..
