Music for Weddings & Events

My aim is to make your day more special than you ever could have hoped for. I provide a highly personalised service which will ensure that you get exactly what you want for your celebration, and will create magical memories for you and your guests for years to come.

Claire Heffernan (MA Music Performance, BMus) performs an exquisite mix of soulful contemporary song, light classical, and a more delicate Celtic sound. She is a classical pianist, Irish harpist and vocalist based in Limerick, performing at weddings and events for the past 10 years throughout Limerick, Clare, Tipperary, Cork, Galway, Kerry, Offaly, and beyond. She strives to create the perfect musical atmosphere for your occasion.

Claire is a classical soprano, pop vocalist, choral singer, and singer-songwriter. Versatility of style is one of her strengths – classical, pop, soul, folk, jazz, Irish, and musical theatre.

Claire performs as a harpist for the world-renowned Bunratty Castle Banquets, as well as in the beloved Bunratty restaurant, Gallaghers. She has performed with the Cork School of Music Project Orchestra, Pfizerpfonics, Cantamus, and has featured as a guest soloist at Carols by Candlelight, Fota House‘s annual festive event.

Claire holds 1st class honours Masters in Music Performance from the CIT Cork School of Music. having specialised in piano accompaniment under Dr. Gabriela Mayer. She also holds a Bachelor of Music with 1st class honours, for which she specialised in solo piano performance under Nicholas O’Halloran, vocal accompaniment, orchestration, and choral conducting.
