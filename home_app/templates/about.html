Welcome!

Claire Heffernan (MA Music Performance, BMus) performs an exquisite mix of soulful contemporary song, light classical, and a more delicate Celtic sound. She is a classical pianist, Irish harpist and vocalist based in Limerick, performing at weddings and events for the past 10 years throughout Limerick, Clare, Tipperary, Cork, Galway, Kerry, Offaly, and beyond. She strives to create the perfect musical atmosphere for your occasion.

Claire is a classical soprano, pop vocalist, choral singer, and singer-songwriter. Versatility of style is one of her strengths – classical, pop, soul, folk, jazz, Irish, musical theatre. She began with 11 years of musical theatre in a stage school, and went on to achieve Grade 8 Voice (ABRSM, with an “outstanding” distinction) under Máire Keary Scanlon, as well as undertaking many years of classical voice training with Robert Craig (MMus Juilliard) at the CIT Cork School of Music.

As a singer, Claire has worked as a vocal coach and choral director (Voiceworks Studio, Cork), a harpist-singer (Knappogue Castle, Shannon Heritage 2007-present), and has been a longstanding member of the internationally-acclaimed chamber choir Voci Nuove (Ireland’s Choir of the Year, 2015 & 2016).

She has performed as a solo vocalist in concert with the CIT Cork School of Music Jazz Big Band; Voci Nuove Choir; Pfizerpfonics Choir; “Take Me Or Leave Me: Hits from the Musicals” at the Half Moon Theatre, Cork; and at the Cork Guiness Jazz Festival as a co-headliner for Voiceworks Jazz Showcase, Imperial Hotel, Cork. On 29th March – 1st April 2017, she will perform the principle role of Florence in Limerick Musical Society‘s Chess at the University Concert Hall, Limerick.

Claire began harp at ten years old, and went on to complete the Royal Irish Academy of Music (RIAM) Grade 8 examination with a high distinction. That same year, in 2007, she achieved the the RIAM Gold Medal for solo harp performance. Since then, she has been one of the principal harpists for Shannon Heritage. For six months every year, she performs for Knappogue Castle in Co. Clare (2007-present).

Claire performs as a harpist for the world-renowned Bunratty Castle Banquets, as well as in the beloved Bunratty restaurant, Gallaghers. She has performed with the Cork School of Music Project Orchestra, Pfizerpfonics, Cantamus, and has featured as a guest soloist at Carols by Candlelight, Fota House‘s annual festive event. She is an in-demand musician, actively taking bookings for private functions, weddings, concerts, and recordings in the Munster area. She is available to collaborate with other singers and instrumentalists. She plays on a beautiful Livia, a 36-string lever harp by Salvi. She can also provide a PA system for harp (microphone, pre-amp, speaker), should a venue require it.
